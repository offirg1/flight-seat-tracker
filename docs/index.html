How this number is computed

Airlines never publish exact remaining-seat counts. This tracker searches the Duffel flight API once a day for every departure date in the window, keeps itineraries that arrive at the destination by the deadline, and counts open seats on each unique destination-bound flight's seat map. Not every airline publishes seat maps (unmapped flights appear in the flight and itinerary counts but add no seats), and a blocked seat isn't necessarily sold. The headline is therefore a floor estimate — real availability is at least this, and the day-over-day trend (flights filling up or opening) is the reliable signal. Flights reachable from more than one origin airport are counted once in the total.
